Privacy Policy - WebNote Mobile App

Effective date: February 12, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how WebNote (“we”, “our”, or “the app”) handles information when you use the WebNote mobile application.

Summary: WebNote does not sell your data. Your saved website links and notes are stored on your device.

1) Information We Collect

WebNote is designed to work without collecting personal information from users. We do not require you to provide your name, phone number, or email to use core features.

  • Notes & Links you create: Stored locally on your device to organize your saved websites.
  • Account / Login: If the app supports sign-in, it is only used for syncing (if enabled).

2) How We Use Information

Information stored in the app (such as website links, titles, folders, and notes) is used only to provide app features:

  • Save and organize website links with notes
  • Group items using folders and colors
  • Search and quickly access your saved items

3) Data Storage

By default, your notes and saved website links are stored on your device. If you enable synchronization features, the app may store your data to provide syncing across devices.

4) Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We do not share your notes and saved links with third parties except:

  • When required by law (legal request)
  • To protect the safety and security of users and the app

5) Third-Party Services

WebNote may use system services required for app functionality (for example, internet access to open websites). If we add third-party services (e.g., analytics, crash reporting, ads), we will update this policy accordingly.

6) Permissions

WebNote may request permissions only to provide features. Examples:

  • Internet: to open website links inside the app or browser
  • Storage (if used): to back up or restore notes (optional)

7) Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information. However, no method of storage is 100% secure. We recommend keeping your device protected with a passcode/lock screen.

8) Children’s Privacy

WebNote is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9) Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will update the “Effective date” at the top of this page.
