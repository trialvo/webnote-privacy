Privacy Policy – WebNote

Effective date: February 12, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how WebNote (“we”, “our”, or “the app”) handles user information when you use the WebNote mobile application.

Important: WebNote respects your privacy. We do not sell or share your personal data.

1. Information We Collect

WebNote is designed to function without collecting personal information. You can use the app without creating an account.

  • User-created content: Website links, notes, folder names, colors, and icons that you create inside the app.
  • Optional sync data: If cloud or backup features are enabled, data may be stored to support synchronization.

2. How We Use Information

Any data stored by the app is used only to provide core functionality:

  • Saving website links with notes
  • Organizing content using folders
  • Searching and managing saved notes
  • Improving app stability and usability

3. Data Storage

By default, all notes and website links are stored locally on your device. WebNote does not transmit your personal content to external servers unless you explicitly enable backup or synchronization features.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent user data. Your information is not shared with third parties except in the following cases:

  • When required by law or legal process
  • To protect the rights, safety, or security of users and the app

5. Third-Party Services

WebNote may use basic system services such as internet connectivity to open website links. If third-party services such as analytics, crash reporting, or advertisements are added in the future, this Privacy Policy will be updated accordingly.

6. App Permissions

WebNote only requests permissions that are necessary for functionality:

  • Internet: To open saved website links
  • Storage (optional): For backup or restore features

7. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information. However, no method of electronic storage is completely secure. We recommend securing your device with a lock screen or password.

8. Children’s Privacy

WebNote is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.
