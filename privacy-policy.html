Privacy Policy – WebNote

Effective date: February 12, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how WebNote (“we”, “our”, or “the app”) handles information when you use the WebNote mobile application.

Summary: WebNote does not sell your data. By default, your notes and saved website links are stored on your device.

1) Information We Collect

WebNote is designed to work without requiring you to provide personal information. You can use the core features without creating an account.

  • User-created content: website links, notes, folder names, colors, icons, and preferences you save in the app.
  • Optional sync/backup: if you enable cloud backup/sync, your saved content may be stored to synchronize across devices.

2) How We Use Information

  • To provide core functionality (save links, add notes, organize by folders)
  • To help you search and manage your saved content
  • To improve stability and usability of the app

3) Data Storage

By default, your content is stored locally on your device. WebNote does not transmit your personal content to external servers unless you explicitly enable backup/synchronization features.

4) Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent user data. We do not share your saved notes/links with third parties except:

  • When required by law or legal process
  • To protect users’ safety and the security of the app

5) Third-Party Services

WebNote may use basic system services (for example, internet access to open website links). If third-party services (analytics, crash reporting, ads) are added later, we will update this policy.

6) Permissions

  • Internet: to open saved links
  • Storage (optional): for backup/restore features (if enabled)

7) Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information. No storage method is 100% secure. Please protect your device with a lock screen.

8) Children’s Privacy

WebNote is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9) Updates to This Policy

We may update this page from time to time. We will update the effective date when changes are made.
